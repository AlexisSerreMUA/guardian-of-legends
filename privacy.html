Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Guardian of Legends ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Guardian of Legends platform, an AI-powered content creation and publishing tool that integrates with TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize our platform via TikTok Login Kit, we receive your TikTok Open ID and display name, which are required to authenticate your account and publish content on your behalf.
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens provided by TikTok are stored locally on your device to maintain your authenticated session.
  • Content Data: AI-generated content (scenarios, images, captions) created through our platform is stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the platform, including publication history and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity via TikTok OAuth 2.0
  • Publish photo carousel content to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • Maintain a local history of published content to avoid duplicates
  • Improve the platform's content generation capabilities

4. TikTok Integration and Data Sharing

Our platform integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. When you authorize our application:

  • We access your TikTok profile information (open_id, display name) via the user.info.basic scope
  • We publish photo carousel content to your TikTok account via the video.publish and video.upload scopes
  • We do not share your TikTok credentials or personal data with any third parties
  • Your data is processed in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

5. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting your data:

  • OAuth access tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers
  • No personal user data is stored on external servers
  • AI-generated content is processed through third-party AI providers (Anthropic, fal.ai) in accordance with their respective privacy policies
  • We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon disconnecting your TikTok account, all associated access tokens are deleted from your device.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to data portability

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok for Developers: For OAuth authentication and content publishing
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For AI-generated content scenarios
  • fal.ai: For AI-generated images
  • GitHub: For temporary image hosting during the publishing process

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: themysteriousguardian@gmail.com
